05 — Motion

Choreography

One CSS ease everywhere — cubic-bezier(.22,.75,.25,1) — and GSAP defaults of 1s power3.out. Transforms and opacity only. Every animation has a reduced-motion story.

Signature ease

Rise-and-fade reveal

y: 40px + fade, ~1s, stagger 0.08s — the entrance for everything (.rv / ScrollTrigger batch at top 88%).

Hover language

Ink veil rises inside buttons; 1px cobalt underline sweeps across inline links; arrows nudge +4px; cards lift.

hello@zoenex.studio Hover the veil

Duration bands

Micro0.2sOpacity, color shifts
Hover0.3–0.45sSweeps, fills, nudges

Reveal0.8–1.15sEntrances, clip reveals

GSAP roles — power4.out headlines · power3.out general · power2.out counters · back.out(1.7) pops · ease:none for scrubbed parallax. Magnetic buttons follow the pointer at 0.28× offset (quickTo, 0.4s). Custom cursor: 10px difference-blend dot growing to 56px, fine pointers only.
