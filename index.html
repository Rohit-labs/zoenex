Before you ask

Common
questions.

How long does a typical project take?

Most websites ship in 4–8 weeks, automation systems in 3–6 weeks, and motion projects in 2–5 weeks depending on scope. You'll get a fixed timeline in the proposal before anything starts.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes — about half our work is international. We're remote-first, overlap with EU hours daily and with US hours by arrangement, and invoice in INR or USD.

Can you take over an existing site or automation?

Usually, yes. We start with a short paid audit of the current build, then give you a plan: improve in place, rebuild in parts, or start fresh — with honest reasoning for each.

What happens after launch?

Every project includes a handover and 30 days of post-launch support. After that, most clients keep us on a monthly retainer for optimisation, new automations and fresh motion content.
